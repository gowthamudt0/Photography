Sandesh kadur Photographer

Red-billed tropicbirds(phaethon aethereus) nest on the rocky grounds
of oceanic islands in the Atlantic and pacific oceans, and the northern
Indian ocean.The largest colonies are found in the Galapagos Islands
and in the Caribbean. They prey on small fish by diving,but have been
observed catching flying fish mid-flight.
